Obtaining

The Mithril-Plated Drill Engine can be created in The Forge after unlocking Heart of the Mountain Tier 3. Forging takes 1 day and costs 2 Drill Motors and 1 Mithril Plate.

Usage

The Mithril-Plated Drill Engine can be applied to a Drill's Drill Engine slot at a Drill Mechanic, granting it +75 Mining Speed and +5 Mining Fortune.

Upgrading

The Mithril-Plated Drill Engine can be upgraded into the Titanium-Plated Drill Engine after reaching Heart of the Mountain Tier 5, granting an additional +75 Mining Speed and +10 Mining Fortune.
